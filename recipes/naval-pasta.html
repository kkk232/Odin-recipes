Naval pasta

The legendary recipe for naval pasta, invented, according to one version, by a Soviet chef in the middle of the last century and presented at a culinary competition in Rome.
The main feature of this recipe is the addition of butter, and at two stages of cooking. The recipe also differs in the method of frying the minced meat and onions.
It is advisable to choose pasta with grooves so that the minced meat adheres well to them.

Ingredients:
  • Pasta - 225g
  • Beef - 275g
  • Onions - 175g
  • Butter - 50g
  • Vegetable oil - 3 spoons
  • Salt - to taste
  • Ground black pepper - to taste
  • Water - 1.25l
Step by step photo recipe:
  1. Prepare products according to the list.
  2. Cut the peeled onion into small cubes. (In the original recipe, the onion is cut into feathers.)
  3. Heat a tablespoon of vegetable oil in a frying pan. Add chopped onion and fry, stirring, for 7-8 minutes until golden brown.
  4. After this, add 25 g of butter to the onion.
  5. Melt the butter and fry the onion for another 1-2 minutes over low heat until a pleasant creamy onion aroma appears.
  6. Cut the beef into pieces and pass through a meat grinder with a medium wire rack.
  7. Place the minced meat in a separate, well-heated frying pan. Continuously break up the minced meat with a spatula so that there are no large lumps.
    Fry over a heat slightly above medium until all the juice has evaporated and the minced meat has changed color to gray, about 5 minutes.
    After this, pour 1 tablespoon of vegetable oil into the frying pan with the minced meat, reduce the heat slightly and fry the minced meat until golden brown and with a pleasant aroma of fried meat, another 7-8 minutes.
    Do not add salt or pepper to the minced meat during frying.
  8. Combine the prepared minced meat and fried onion. Add salt and pepper, stir and leave to infuse for a while.
  9. Place the boiled pasta in a colander to drain all the water, then return it to the pan.
  10. Add 25g of butter to the pasta. Stir.
  11. Add minced meat and onion to the pasta in the pan. Mix everything well.
  12. Place the navy-style macaroni on a plate and serve.
