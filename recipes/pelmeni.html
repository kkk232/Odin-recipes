Pelmeni

Pelmeni are traditional Russian dumplings made with a simple dough of flour, water, eggs, and salt.
Typically, they are stuffed with a filling of ground pork, chicken, turkey, or beef seasoning with onions and salt and pepper.
Largely considered the national dish of Russia, you will find these tasty dumplings served in homes, cafes, and fancy restaurants throughout the country.

Ingredients:
  • Dough of flour
  • Water
  • Salt
  • Eggs
  • Chicken
How to Make Pelmeni by Hand:
  1. Roll the Dough Out: Using a rolling pin, roll out the dough into a flat piece. Next, use a circular cookie cutter to cut out small circles from the dough.
  2. Shape the Dumplings:Top each dough circle with a spoonful of the meat mixture.
    Fold over the sides to create a half-moon shape. Next, pinch the pointy edges together, securing them tightly with your fingers.
    Dust with flour and freeze for later or boil to eat straight away!
