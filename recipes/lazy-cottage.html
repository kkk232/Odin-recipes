Lazy cottage

Lazy cottage cheese dumplings are a wonderful, healthy, quick, and most importantly, delicious breakfast. Children eat them with pleasure.

Ingredients:
  • Cottage cheese - 500g
  • Eggs - 1-2
  • Sugar (optional) - 2-3
  • Flour - 1-1.5 cups
  • Butter - 3 spoons
  • Sour cream - to taste
  • Salt - 1 pinch
Step by step recipe
  1. Pass the homemade cottage cheese through a meat grinder or rub it through a sieve
  2. Add eggs, sugar (optional), melted butter, salt and mix well
  3. Then add sifted flour to the resulting mass and knead the curd dough. Put a pan of water on the fire. While we are fiddling with the dough, it will just boil
  4. Place the curd dough on a floured table and cut into 2 equal parts. Roll each part into a thin sausage shape, flatten slightly, and cut into small pieces
  5. Place lazy dumplings in salted boiling water and cook until they float to the surface
  6. Place the finished vareniki on a plate with a slotted spoon and pour melted butter over them. Serve lazy vareniki with sour cream
